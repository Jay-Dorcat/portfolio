About Me

This is where you can learn a bit more about me!

I'm currently a hobbyist Game Developer and primarily I am a Programmer and Designer for my games!
I want to make games because they let people experience things they'd never get to experience otherwise.
Even before I decided to study and work towards being a game developer,
I already had an interest in computers and programming so I already had a headstart.

I studied Computer Science and Game Development, and participate in Game jams when I can,
though I often work on games and projects for myself to test and push my skills.
I work hard and want to learn whatever I need to help create the next best games!

When not creating games I like to play games, both to have fun and to get inspiration and ideas!
I'm a big fan of sandbox, logistical, strategy and rogue-like games,
although I also like to try genres I'm not as familiar with,
trying genres I'm not familiar with helps me to expand my understanding of what games can be.
